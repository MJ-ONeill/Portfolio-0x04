Challenges

The struggles I faced were minor, one of them being the styling. The colors I initially picked did not work well together, and the font choice made the text either unclear or harsh on the eyes. The main issue was with the background color, which frustrated me. However, I decided to take a step back, grab a cup of coffee, and reconsider my color scheme for a successful outcome. I opted for a light and dark combination, with subtle hints of color instead of overwhelming the design with excessive colors

Another frustration was adding the static files to the Python Django framework. I prefer separating my JavaScript (JS) and CSS files from my HTML files using folders. This meant that I had to make the necessary changes in views.py, settings.py, and urls.py to ensure a successful setup
