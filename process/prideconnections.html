My motivation for creating PrideConnections..

I Decided to make a LGBTQ events web app for the LGBTQ community to have a space to share and find events close to them in one place.
It's important to me because I am also part of that community and Know first hand how difficult it is to find Pride events happening in and around my town, And sharing a event on social media does not reach as much people as we would liike to think,
Example like the the pride weekend even the Pride parade/walk that was happenning in June 2023 was shared all over social media like Facebook, Whatsapp, Insta etc yet still was ther some poeple who did not know about the events happening that weekend and some of those who knew did not really know when and where exactly especially since our own only recently started celebrating Pride month and LGBTQ related events.
